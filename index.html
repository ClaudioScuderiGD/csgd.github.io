Hi

I’m Linus Jernström, a game designer from Dalarna, Sweden.

I am currently pursuing my passion for game design at Playgroundsquad, a renowned game development school in Sweden. I've had a love for video games ever since I started watching my dad play them in my early childhood. His playthroughs of Diablo 3 and Age of Empires 3 are still fond memories of mine.

As a passionate learner and team player, I thrive on challenging myself and others to grow and explore new concepts in game design. My innate curiosity drives me to delve into all aspects of the industry, while my love for problem-solving and teamwork fuels my motivation to overcome obstacles.

I am currently seeking an internship opportunity for the upcoming autumn semester to gain hands-on experience in the gaming industry and contribute to creating engaging and innovative gaming experiences. I am open to working on diverse projects, and I am eager to collaborate with like-minded professionals in the industry. If your company is searching for an enthusiastic, curious and hardworking intern, I would be thrilled to join your team and learn from your expertise.
